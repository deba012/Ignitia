ABOUT THE FEST

ABOUT IGNITIA 2026

IGNITIA 2026 is the annual techno-cultural fest of Pranveer Singh Institute of Technology (PSIT). The fest brings together students from different colleges to participate in technical competitions, cultural events, and interactive activities.

Spread across three days, IGNITIA focuses on innovation, creativity, teamwork, and campus spirit. It is a platform where students learn, compete, perform, and celebrate together.
